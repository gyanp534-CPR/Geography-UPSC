Q.4 Climatology Medium PYQ 2019
Consider the following statements about the Indian Ocean Dipole (IOD):

1. A positive IOD is associated with above normal monsoon rainfall over India.
2. IOD is measured by the difference in Sea Surface Temperatures between the western and eastern Indian Ocean.
3. A negative IOD event was observed in 2019, leading to one of the worst droughts in India.

Which of the above statements are correct?
A 1 and 2 only
B 2 and 3 only
C 1 and 2 only — Statements 1 & 2 are correct; 3 is incorrect
D 1, 2 and 3
Explanation: Statement 1 is correct — positive IOD brings warmer SSTs in the western Indian Ocean and cooler in the east, enhancing moisture flow into India. Statement 2 is correct — IOD index is indeed measured by SST gradient. Statement 3 is incorrect — 2019 actually saw a positive IOD, which partly compensated for a weak El Niño and contributed to a decent monsoon. The negative IOD statement is factually reversed.
